Insured Declared Value or IDV, is calculated as the invoice price of your vehicle minus depreciation. The Invoice price is based on the make, model and variant of your vehicle. Depreciation is calculated as per a standard table approved by the Motor Tariff Act. The older your vehicle the higher the depreciation and therefore lower the IDV as compared to the original invoice price. IDV is the amount the insurer will pay if your vehicle is completely damaged or stolen. IDV, therefore, works as the Sum Insured and the Insurer calculates the premium based on the IDV.
