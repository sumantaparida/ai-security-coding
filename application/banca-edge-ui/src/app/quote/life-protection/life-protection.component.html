Protection
{{ needHideCritical }}

Every person must have a Protection policy. The only question is how much. These policies provide for cover in case of death or disability due to an accident. There are many factors to be considered before you estimate how much cover you must buy: number of dependants, the kind of lifestyle you want to provide to your family, what are your needs for children's education or other needs, your affordability and so on. A simple rule of thumb is an amount approximately 12 to 15 times your current income.
